About Us

We Are ponicone

We at ponicone believe food is medicine. Our bodies are designed to heal themselves if given the right care. If we feed our systems with right food exercise and sleep we never have to rely on any external help. The power of Organic food is immense. It’s life changing and a major solution to many health problems we face. We are grateful to have this opportunity to grow organic food with an ancient technique. But we take more pride in growing food by using 90% less water than regular farming and no soil. Sounds impossible right ? It’s not. This is possible with aquaponics. Aquaponics is an ancient technique used back in the day to grow plants and raise fish. What’s more fascinating is how we can even grow paddy crops with this technic. We take the responsibility of taking care of our planet. We are aware of how we are running out of natural resources and how in the coming years we might go through food crises due to increased population.

WHAT WE DO

Our Services

How is our food organic? Aquaponics; A beautiful symbiosis of plant and fish. In this method of farming, plants and fish coexist. Fish releases the nutrients plants need and plants in return filter the water the fish live in. The water is recycled. Which allows us to grow plants and raise fish without using any excess water or any other fossil fuel that needs to be used in regular farming. We are benefited in every way possible. But what about the fish? If that’s your question then we do not promise to give you a solution cause the only solution is to not to consume them in the first place. But we are not structured in that way as a society for a lot of reasons. We understand people’s choice and respect it. From our side what we can do is alter things that are in our power. We raise fish by ourselves. Which allows us to not to disturb the oceans or the aquatic life, eliminating water pollution, waste of foil fuels and damaging the oceans with waste and plastic.
